Features

Everything you need

From strain to harvest – a tool that grows with your grow.

🌿

Strain Management

Create strains with breeder, genetics, effects, aroma, THC/CBD, and flowering time – including image gallery.

🌱

Seed Tracking

Track seeds with quantity, source, and purchase date. Sort, search, edit inline, and export as PDF.

🌱

Grow Journal

Document week by week with fertilizer, watering, light cycle, and temperature. Includes photos per week.

📷

Image Gallery & Lightbox

Add images to grows, weeks, and harvests. Gallery view with lightbox and arrow key navigation.

🍅

Harvest Tracking

Log harvest date, weight, and notes. With PDF export for your complete grow documentation.

🔒

2FA & User Management

Two-factor authentication via TOTP. First user becomes admin and can control registration.

🌐

Multi-language

English and German built in. Each user can switch the language in their profile.

📈

Dashboard & Search

All your metrics at a glance. Global full-text search queries strains and grows simultaneously.

Privacy

Your data belongs to you

No cloud, no surveillance, no compromises. Homegrow DB runs on your server, under your control.

🗝

100 % Self-Hosted

Install the app on your own server, NAS, or Raspberry Pi. A single Docker container is all you need.

🔐

Fully encrypted

Passwords are hashed with bcrypt. Optional 2FA via TOTP. The database stays local on your machine.

📖

Open Source (MIT)

The entire source code is on GitHub. Audit what the app does. Fork it, customize it.

📈

No Tracking

No analytics, no cookies, no third-party services. Just you and your data.
